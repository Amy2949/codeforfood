Cinnamon Apple Cake

Igredients

  • 1 apple, peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice, fresh squeezed
  • 1 cup creamy roasted almond butter
  • ¼ cup honey or agave nectar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Method
