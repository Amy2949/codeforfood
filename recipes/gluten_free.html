Spinach and onion tortilla

Igredients

  • 1 large Spanish onion, halved and thinly sliced
  • 5 medium free-range eggs, beaten
  • 250g fresh spinach leaves, washed and chopped
  • 40g Cheddar, grated

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan with an ovenproof handle and fry the onion until soft and pale golden. Add the spinach and saut√© for a couple of minutes to wilt the leaves.
  2. Pour the egg mixture into the pan, turn the heat down to its lowest setting and cook the tortilla, uncovered, for about 8 minutes, until there is only a little runny egg left on the top.
  3. Sprinkle the grated cheese over the top and melt under the preheated grill for 1-2 minutes, until the top is golden and bubbling.
